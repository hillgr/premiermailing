Leadership

Pete McConney

President and CEO

Peter E. McConney is the President/CEO of Premier Mail & Fulfillment, LLC.  Prior to purchasing Premier Mail in 2007, Peter served twenty-eight years as a Criminal Investigator with the United States Treasury Department retiring in 2006 as the Special Agent in Charge of the agency’s Chicago Field Division. Upon entering the world of entrepreneurship, Peter quickly recognized the importance of continuing the high quality service provided by Premier Mail’s previous owners since 1994.

As President, Peter has expanded Premier Mail’s production capabilities by adding equipment and doubling its staff.  He also formed a strategic alliance with other companies like Bramkamp Printing, Dana Graphics, Everything’s Image and DocuPros to create the Graphic Village, a collaboration of graphic arts companies located under one roof to offer clients single source expertise in all elements of graphic design, digital and commercial print, direct mail marketing and embroider apparel.

Although busy with his responsibilities at Premier Mail, Peter still finds time to volunteer at the Ronald McDonald House. He also serves on various boards and committees with other non-profit organizations like Josh Cares Charities, Envision Children and the Urban League of Southwest Ohio.  He holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Business Administration and Criminal Justice from the University of Massachusetts and resides in Loveland, Ohio with his three children, Whitney, Taylor and Jake.

Amanda Carter

Vice President

Amanda Carter is the Vice President of Premier Mail & Fulfillment.  Amanda began with Premier Mail in 2008 as a Production Assistant before elevating her career through a succession of increasingly responsible positions within the company.  In 2012, Amanda was promoted to her current position as Vice President where she is in charge of all company production within Premier Mail.

Amanda is a graduate of Lincoln College where she earned her Associate’s Degree in Information Technology.  She resides in Sharonville, Ohio with her two daughters, Jasmine and McKayla.
